How it works

Three things AURA does differently from a typical chat window.

Speaks and listens

Press the mic and talk instead of typing. AURA transcribes what you say and reads its replies back to you out loud.

Remembers context

Mention your name, your job, or a preference once — AURA carries it into future conversations without you repeating it.

Keeps every thread

Every conversation is saved and searchable in your sidebar, so you can pick a topic back up days later.
